Who is nefola?

Teal in Tandem is the internet user known as nefola. We are a plural system, if you do not know what that means here is a resource that we like. We are a trans woman in our early 20's.

I make a variety of writing, code, and music. Sometimes I draw and make video games. We are generally passionate about homestuck, challenging video games, obtuse metrics of narrative assessment, and women.

This short blurb about us is not a substitute for observation or interaction. It is an introduction and a method of expression.
